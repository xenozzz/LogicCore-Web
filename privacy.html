Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 17, 2025

1. Introduction

LogicCore ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you visit our website (regardless of where you visit it from) and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

2. Data We Collect

We are a static website. We do not use cookies for tracking, analytics, or advertising. The only personal data we collect is what you voluntarily provide via our contact form:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Message content

3. How We Use Your Data

Data submitted via our contact form is processed by Web3Forms and delivered directly to our secure email inbox. We use this data solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiries.
  • Provide quotes or service estimates.

We do not share, sell, or rent your data to third parties.

4. Your Legal Rights

Under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR), you have rights including:

  • Your right of access - You have the right to ask us for copies of your personal information.
  • Your right to erasure - You have the right to ask us to erase your personal information in certain circumstances.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at hello@logiccore.cloud.

5. Hosting

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect server logs (IP addresses) for security and maintenance purposes. This is standard internet infrastructure operation.
